I have never heard of kairos before. Now that you have explained it, it seems like a very common rhetorical strategy used in advertisements. I often see time limits used in online shops to encourage users to spend money on a temporary sale. Grocery store weekly fliers are also an example because they reset every week. Customers may feel like they have to do a weekly shopping trip just to take advantage of the sales. In terms of the election, I see this strategy used a lot. I was asked to vote by a woman here on campus, and she tried to convey that the timeframe was short to register. I think you had good depth in your analysis, and you covered enough examples of the kairos rhetorical strategy. You covered the idea of power dynamics because kairos is a power dynamic. If I had to choose an area of improvement, I think the format of the document could have been more decorated.
