The Influence of Friends

Variation across Contexts


In this video, I spoke about how friends can negatively influence dietary choices. I did this by analyzing a video from social media. In my analysis, I mentioned the possibility that this concept could vary depending on cultural norms. I have heard of cultural etiquette involving whether or not one should eat all the food off their plate. There are some occasions where it could be considered rude to decline food. Despite this, I argue that these situations could be avoided with good communication. I follow a strict way of eating, but my friends are very understanding. I have never felt guilted into eating unhealthy foods.

The Blank Stare of Crissy Damm

Writing and Power


I stumbled upon this photo simply on Google Images, but I realized that the photography of Mary Ellen Mark is very powerful. She mainly photographed people who were "away from mainstream society and toward its more interesting, often troubled fringes" (Andrew Long). Writing about such a topic involves care and respect because the subjects of the photographs often have tough lives. In this particular photo, Crissy Damm is a child in a homeless family. Crissy is an example of someone with a lack of power. She has little control over her poverty due to her age, education, and financial position.
